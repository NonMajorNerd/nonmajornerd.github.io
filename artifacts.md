## Overview
> An Artifact is an item your character can equip which modifies their stats. A pool of discoverable Artifacts is generated every stream. Characters can hunt for Artifacts from this pool by purchasing Artifact Maps.
>
>A character may only have one artifact in their posession at a time. If a second artifact is discovered, they must choose to either [keep](/commands.html#keeparchetype-1) their current artifact or replace it with the newly found artifact.
